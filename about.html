Frequently Asked Questions

What is CropAi?
CropAi is an AI-powered agricultural technology platform that helps farmers monitor crops, detect diseases, predict yields, and optimize farming practices using artificial intelligence, machine learning, and remote sensing technologies
How does CropAi work?
CropAi collects data from images, drones, sensors, and satellites. Using advanced AI algorithms, we analyze this data to provide real-time insights into crop health, potential diseases, soil conditions, and weather impact. Farmers receive personalized recommendations to improve crop productivity and reduce risks.
Who can use CropAi?
CropAi is designed for: Individual farmers Agribusinesses Agricultural cooperatives Research institutions Government agricultural departments Whether you have a small farm or large-scale agricultural operations, CropAi can be customized to fit your needs.
